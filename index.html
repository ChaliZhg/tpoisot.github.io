I’m Tim Poisot. I do (mostly theoretical) research on ecological networks and community ecology, write papers, and blog. I also release free open-source software and datasets.

I’m currently a post-doctoral scientist in the group of Dominique Gravel at the Université du Québec à Rimouski. I’m involved in the Québec Centre for Biodiversity Sciences, the French Ecological Society, and the International Network of Next-Generation Ecologists.
