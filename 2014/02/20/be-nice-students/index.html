Be nice to students

20 Feb 2014

You will never do research. You may work as a lab tech, but being a researcher is out of the question for you. Trust me.

I have been told this exact thing by a well known, well published, highly respected scientist (whose identity is not important) at the end of my Bachelor’s degree. I’m certainly glad I didn’t trust him. I’m glad I found people that gave me the opportunity (and training, because Oh boy is it not an innate ability) to do research. And of course, I enjoy the poetic justice there is in the fact that some of the research I produced is good enough that he cited it once or twice.
